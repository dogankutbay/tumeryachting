Yacht charter destination - BODRUM, TURKEY

Discover Southern Turkey on a yacht charter from Bodrum and explore many breathtaking destinations such as Marmaris, Fethiye, Gocek as well as charming seaside villages and isolated small islands of many sizes. Why not embark from Bodrum on a beautiful journey to the neighboring Greek Islands of Rhodes, Kos, Symi in the Dodencanese, and even further to the Cyclades. There are many superb Turkish sailboats called gulets available throughout the year for these splendid yacht cruises in the Eastern Mediterranean and Aegean seas.

As neighbors to Turkey, Greece is an ideal addition to your itinerary and offers plenty of islands for an exciting island hopping experience with family and friends. With our great selection of online and in-house yachts, there is bound to be a luxury gulet charter that is just right for your group.
